## ‚ö†Ô∏è Safety & Best Practices

### üî• Electrical Safety Reminders

1. **Turn Off Power** ‚Äì Always disconnect power before working on electrical circuits.
2. **Use Proper PPE** ‚Äì Insulated gloves, safety glasses, and appropriate gear.
3. **Follow NEC Guidelines** ‚Äì Ensure compliance with local and national codes.
4. **Double-Check Connections** ‚Äì Prevent shorts and miswiring.
5. **Test Before Touching** ‚Äì Use a multimeter to confirm power is off.
